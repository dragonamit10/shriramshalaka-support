Shri RamShalaka Prashnawali

About Shri RamShalaka

Shri RamShalaka Prashnawali is a traditional devotional practice associated with the Ramcharitmanas. This app presents the experience in a simple offline format designed for iPhone users. It is intended for personal spiritual reflection and should not be considered a substitute for personal judgment, professional advice, or religious guidance.

Ancient wisdom from the Ramcharitmanas presented in a beautifully designed iPhone application.
